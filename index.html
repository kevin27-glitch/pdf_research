Our Story

KK-Stuengine was founded to make quality study resources simple, accessible, and organized for students everywhere. We believe learning should be easier, and revision should not be a stressful search.

Our Team

Founder:
Kevin Wang
Kevin is the founder and chief editor of KK-Stuengine. He is a student at Li Po Chun United World College (LPCUWC) in Hong Kong, passionate about education, technology, and student welfare. He built this platform to help students save time, balance their studies more effectively, and access well-structured revision materials in one place. His goal is to create a free, clean, and reliable study engine for every student.

Co-Founder:
Kyle Tsang
Kyle is an academic enthusiast with a strong interest in aerospace engineering, innovation, and problem-solving. He supports the platform with technical insights, content curation, and the vision of making education more efficient. Kyle believes that well-organized resources can greatly improve learning efficiency and empower students to explore their potential while staying focused and balanced.

Together, we aim to build a trusted study companion built by students, for students.
